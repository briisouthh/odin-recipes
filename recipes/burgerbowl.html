Burger Bowl

Our healthy version of a burger. Packed with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, lettuce, and thousand island sauce. This recipe will keep your cravings at bay while keeping you full.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Romaine
  • Thousand Island

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes and onions to small slices and set to the side
  2. Prepare ground beef on skillet
  3. Cut up romaine lettuce
  4. Add everything to the bowl and top with thousand island sauce
